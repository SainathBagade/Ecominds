EcoMinds operates on a tiered access system, ensuring a structured and secure educational environment for all participants.

### 1. Student Experience (The Learners)
*   **Onboarding**: New users register and select their grade level (4â€“12).
*   **Learning Hubs**: Students access specialized modules like *Environmental Science*, *Climate Physics*, and *Sustainable Chemistry*.
*   **Gamification**: Every lesson completed and quiz cleared earns **EcoPoints**. 
*   **Progression**: Users unlock **Badges** (e.g., "Eco Warrior") and climb the **Global Leaderboard**.
*   **Competitions**: Students enter time-limited "Eco-Battles" to test their knowledge against peers worldwide.

### 2. Teacher Experience (The Mentors)
*   **Specialized Control**: Teachers are assigned to one of three core subjects by the Admin.
*   **Hub Management**: They monitor student engagement within their specific specialization.
*   **Challenge Creation**: Teachers create and manage environmental challenges and missions for their students.
*   **Performance Monitoring**: Teachers have a dedicated dashboard to track class-wide impact and completion rates.

### 3. Admin Experience (The Coordinators)
*   **School Governance**: Admins manage a specific school or organization within the platform.
*   **Staff Onboarding**: They have the exclusive authority to create teacher accounts and assign their specializations.
*   **Student Oversite**: Manage student registrations and maintain the integrity of their school's node.
*   **Analytics**: View high-level metrics on school-wide environmental literacy and engagement.

### 4. Super Admin (The Global Commander)
*   **Platform Control**: Maintains the global network and server health.
*   **Organization Management**: Authorizes new schools and organizations to join the EcoMinds network.
*   **Direct Delegation**: Directly creates and manages school-level administrators.
